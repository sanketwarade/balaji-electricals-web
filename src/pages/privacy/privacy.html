Your Privacy is Important to Us

At BALAJI ELECTRICALS, we prioritize your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information in compliance with applicable Indian laws.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and other relevant details when you contact us or use our website. For certain services, we may collect sensitive personal data, including financial information (if applicable), as defined under Indian law.

How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Providing you with better services and communication.
  • Fulfilling contractual obligations or service requests.
  • Improving our products, solutions, and user experience.
  • Ensuring compliance with legal obligations under Indian law.

Data Protection and Storage

Your data is securely stored using reasonable security practices as required by the **IT Rules, 2011**. We ensure that:

  • Only authorized personnel have access to your data.
  • All data is protected against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.
  • Retention policies are followed, and data is not kept longer than necessary.

Third-Party Sharing

Your data will not be shared with third parties without your explicit consent unless required by:

  • Legal obligations under the **Information Technology Act, 2000** or other applicable laws.
  • Government requests or law enforcement agencies.

Your Rights

As per the upcoming **Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023**, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: You can request a copy of your data held by us.
  • Right to Rectification: You can request corrections to inaccurate data.
  • Right to Erasure: You can request the deletion of your data, subject to legal obligations.
  • Right to Consent Management: You can withdraw or modify your consent for data usage.

Legal Compliance

This privacy policy complies with:

  • The **Information Technology Act, 2000** and its amendments.
  • The **IT Rules, 2011** (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information Rules).
  • The forthcoming **Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023**.

Grievance Redressal

If you have any concerns regarding your privacy or data, you may contact us.
